This page contains interactive visualization of the Scottish Witchcraft Dataset. You can read more about the dataset and all of its features << HERE >>. We divided the data in three levels as follows:
♦ Level 1 contains data for people who are accused in Witchcraft. Not all of them have Case and Trial information. You can see their occupation, gender, residency.
♦ Level 2 contains Cases of accused people in Witchcraft. Data includes information about demonic types, rituals (drinks and food during them, dancing, singing), commission, complaints, confessions and other data.
♦ Level 3 contains Trial data: defense, verdict, sentsense, execution and others.
Select the level you want to explore from the menu at the top
